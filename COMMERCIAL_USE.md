## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I charge my company to develop SpinBot integrations?
A: Internal consulting/development work within your organization is fine. Selling SpinBot-specific services to external clients is not permitted.

### Q: Can I include SpinBot in my open-source project?
A: Yes, as long as your project is also non-commercial and properly attributes SpinBot.

### Q: Can I donate to the project?
A: Voluntary donations or sponsorships to support development are welcome, but do not grant commercial rights.

### Q: Can a consultant deploy SpinBot for my company?
A: Yes, but the consultant cannot charge specifically for SpinBot itself - only for their time/services.

### Q: Can we use SpinBot in a non-profit organization?
A: Yes! Non-profit and charitable organizations can use SpinBot freely.

### Q: What if I want to commercialize a modified version?
A: You must obtain a commercial license or create something substantially different that doesn't use our code.

### Q: Can we offer SpinBot to our clients as part of our consulting?
A: Only if it's a free add-on to your services. You cannot charge for SpinBot itself or bundle it into paid offerings.
